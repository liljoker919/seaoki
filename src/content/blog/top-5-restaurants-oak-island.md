# Top 5 Restaurants on Oak Island, NC

Oak Island is more than just miles of gorgeous, uncrowded beaches—it's also home to some fantastic dining that locals swear by and visitors quickly fall in love with. Whether you're looking for a laid-back waterfront spot or a cozy family dinner after a day in the sun, these five restaurants are must-visits during your stay.

Planning a full evening out? [Check out the 2026 Summer Concert Schedule at Middleton Park](/blog/oak-island-summer-concerts-2026/) and pair a great meal with free live music every Friday night all summer long.

## 1. Provision Company

A true Oak Island icon. Provision Company sits right on the Intracoastal Waterway and serves up incredibly fresh seafood in a casual, no-frills atmosphere. Order at the counter, grab a picnic table outside, and watch the boats go by while you dig into a steaming bowl of clam chowder or a perfectly fried shrimp basket.

**Why we love it:** The sunsets over the water are unbeatable, and the hush puppies are legendary.

**Tip:** Arrive early or expect a wait—this place is popular for good reason.

---

## 2. Lonerider at Oak Island

Craving craft beer alongside your meal? Lonerider Brewing Co. has a taproom on Oak Island that pairs their award-winning brews with a rotating menu of hearty eats. It's a great spot for groups, with a relaxed vibe and plenty of indoor and outdoor seating.

**Why we love it:** The beer is outstanding, the staff is friendly, and there's usually live music on weekends.

**Tip:** Check their social media for food truck schedules and live music events.

## 3. Salt 64

Salt 64 brings a slightly elevated dining experience to Oak Island without the pretension. Their menu focuses on fresh, locally sourced ingredients, with standout dishes like seared scallops, creative tacos, and a killer brunch on weekends. The cocktails are worth the trip alone.

**Why we love it:** Perfect for a date night or a special family dinner after a week of beach cooking.

**Tip:** Make reservations during peak summer season—it fills up fast.

---

## 4. Paul's of Oak Island

Paul's is the kind of neighborhood gem that keeps locals coming back year after year. Known for generous portions and comfort food done right, it's an excellent choice for a family meal. The menu spans from fresh seafood to burgers and sandwiches, so even the pickiest eater in your group will find something to love.

**Why we love it:** Friendly service, honest prices, and portions big enough to fuel a full day at the beach.

**Tip:** Don't skip the she-crab soup—it's one of the best on the island.

---

## 5. The Seaside Mermaid

With its breezy coastal decor and welcoming atmosphere, The Seaside Mermaid is a fan favorite for everything from casual lunches to sunset dinners. Their seafood selection is fresh and plentiful, and they do a fantastic job with vegetarian and lighter fare too.

**Why we love it:** A great spot for the whole family, with options for everyone and a warm, beachy vibe.

**Tip:** Try to snag a table on the porch when the weather is nice.
